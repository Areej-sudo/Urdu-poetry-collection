About Mirza Ghalib

Mirza Ghalib (1797-1869) is one of the most famous and influential poets of Urdu and Persian literature. His poetry explores themes of love, loss, philosophy, and the meaning of life. His ghazals are still widely appreciated and studied across the world.

Ghalib's poetry reflects deep emotional insights combined with a mastery of the Urdu language. His unique style continues to influence modern-day poetry.
