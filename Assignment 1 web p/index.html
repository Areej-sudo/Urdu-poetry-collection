What is Urdu Poetry?

Urdu poetry is a form of artistic expression that touches the soul through its intricate use of metaphors, emotions, and themes. With centuries of history, Urdu poets have crafted verses that explore love, loss, life, and the divine.

"Dil dhoondta hai phir wahi fursat ke raat din."

- Mirza Ghalib
